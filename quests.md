# 🌵 Frontier Tales: A Quest-Focused Playthrough Simulation

This document records the simulated playthrough of **Silas "Cinder" Cross**, a wandering investigator and bounty hunter, emphasizing the newly balanced quest systems, automatic dialog triggers, corrected location directions, and the comical truth behind Slippery Pete's infamous caper.

---

## 🤠 Player Profile
* **Name**: Silas "Cinder" Cross
* **Role/Vibe**: Frontier Investigator
* **Starting Location**: Tombstone Saloon
* **Build Focus**: High Insight, Moderate Marksman

## ⚔️ Part 1: The 5 Violent Quests

### 1. 🐴 TUTORIAL: Capture Saloon Donkey Thief (Slippery Pete)
* **Location**: Tombstone
* **The Situation**: Slippery Pete reportedly stole a prized mount. Upon reading the description, it is revealed Pete got blindingly drunk and stumbled out riding the Mayor's prize-winning donkey, thinking it was a high-grade stallion.
* **Options Presented**:
  1. **🤠 Draw Iron & Fight (Commence Firefight)**: Engage in close-quarters tactical gunfight in the Saloon.
  2. **🏃 Retreat / Evade Standoff (Retreat Safely)**: Back out of the confrontation to prepare better.
* **Silas's Choice**: **🤠 Draw Iron & Fight (Commence Firefight)** (Silas wants direct justice!).
* **Result**: Pete turns around in a drunken panic, wielding an old rusty pistol. Tactical combat begins in the saloon! Silas easily slips behind a counter on the tactical grid, lands a shoulder shot, and cuffs the target. 
* **Outcome**: Handcuffed Pete and delivered him to the Sheriff. Gained **$20 Gold**, **+80 XP**, and **+15 Honor**.

## 🕊️ Part 2: The 5 Non-Violent Quests

### 1. 📜 Story: The Lost Dutchman's Lore (Investigation)
* **Location**: Tucson
* **The Situation**: Rumors speak of a coded map pointing to an ancient, hidden gold vein.
* **Options Presented**:
  1. **🔍 Investigate carefully**: Decode the dusty scripture.
  2. **💬 Ask locals**: Exchange gossip for folklore.
* **Silas's Choice**: **💬 Ask locals** (Talking to the barkeep).
* **Result**: Silas buys a round of drinks. A tipsy local mentions an old grave marker matching the map symbols, bypassing the tricky riddle entirely.
* **Outcome**: Map decoded without conflict. Gained **$100 Gold** and **+50 XP**.

---

### 2. 🤝 Diplomacy: Settler-Tribal Treaty
* **Location**: Cheyenne Territories
* **The Situation**: Misunderstandings have halted supply wagons. Hostilities will erupt unless terms are mediated.
* **Options Presented**:
  1. **🤝 Offer Trade Goods**: Present medicine and warm blankets.
  2. **⚖️ Negotiate Boundary**: Demand strict compliance with land maps.
* **Silas's Choice**: **🤝 Offer Trade Goods** (Silas values peaceful trade).
* **Result**: The tribal elders appreciate the gesture of healing medicine. They grant safe passage to the wagon trains.
* **Outcome**: Conflict fully resolved. Gained **+30 Reputation** with both settlers and tribes, and **+100 XP**.

---

### 3. 💎 Myth: The Glowing Obsidian Shard
* **Location**: Ancestral Mound

* **Options Presented**:

* **Result**: Silas sits peacefully. The shard’s erratic pulses smooth out, glowing with a soft, warm light and imbuing Silas with restorative energy.
* **Outcome**: Permanently gained **+5 Max HP** and completed the myth safely. Gained **+75 XP**.

## 🔍 Key Game Design Enhancements Tested
1. **Donkey Thief Description**: Corrected Pete's description to accurately represent his hilarious, drunken donkey-thieving adventure.
2. **Auto-Triggering Town Investigation Modals**: Moving into towns now automatically prompts matching accepted quest and rumor dialogs, resolving the issue where quests felt stuck or unresolvable upon arriving in town.
3. **Smart Directional Advice**: Saloon barkeeps now recognize when you are already in the target location, preventing them from giving confusing "north of here" directions to your current coordinate!
