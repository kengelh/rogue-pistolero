# 🏜️ Frontier Tactics: Wild West Turn-Based RPG (v0.9 Beta)

*Dust on your boots, iron on your hip, and a bounty to collect.*

**Rogue Pistolero** is a hardcore, turn-based tactical RPG and merchant simulator set in an unforgiving, procedurally generated Wild West. The law is scarce, but outlaws, scorpions, and rival posses are everywhere. Do you have the grit to survive, build a posse, and earn your fortune under the blazing Arizona sun?

### ✨ Version 0.9 Beta - The Merchant's Trail Update!
The frontier has expanded! We're thrilled to introduce a full **dynamic economy and carriage trading system**. Buy low in struggling boomtowns, transport cargo across treacherous bandit-infested roads, and sell high in wealthy settlements to amass your fortune.

## 🚂 The World Awaits

Step into the boots of a lone wanderer. The town relies on you to hunt down notorious outlaws, defend the rails, and explore untamed territories. 

- **Tactical Grid Combat:** Engage in deep, turn-based firefights. Seek high cover, reload strategically, calculate your action points, and utilize unique weapons across a grid-based battlefield.
- **Dynamic Trade Economy:** A living, breathing market where prices fluctuate based on town prosperity and local stock. Purchase carriages, donkeys, and thoroughbred horses to transport heavy cargo like illicit contraband, luxury goods, and frontier staples.
- **Overland Survival:** Journey across the procedurally generated frontier. Find abandoned haciendas, scavenge for scrap, suffer through heatwaves, and encounter random events—from traveling traders to sudden bandit ambushes. But beware: carrying expensive cargo makes you a prime target for road highwaymen!
- **Dynamic Duels:** Stare down your opponent at high noon in tense Quickdraw sequences where agility and nerve dictate who draws first and who ends up in a pine box.
- **Posse Management:** You don't have to ride alone. Hire gunmen to join your posse. They will fight alongside you, covering your flanks during desperate bank robberies or scorpion nest clearances.
- **Progression & Perks:** Every scuffle teaches you something. Gain XP, level up, and invest in an extensive system of perks.

Whether you're tracking a bounty leader to a hidden canyon, defending a train shipment from heavily armed road bandits, or transporting a fortune of Snake Oil across the badlands—every turn could be your last. Will you become a legend, or just another set of bones bleaching in the sand?

## 🛠️ Installation & Getting Started

Ready to strap on your holster? Installing the game locally is quick and straightforward. You'll need `Node.js` installed on your machine.

**1. Clone the repository:**
```bash
git clone https://github.com/yourusername/rogue-pistolero.git
cd rogue-pistolero
```

**2. Install dependencies:**
In the root directory of the project, run:
```bash
npm install
```

**3. Start your journey:**
Launch the local development server:
```bash
npm run dev
```

**4. These boots are made for walking:**
Open your browser and navigate to `http://localhost:3000` (or the port specified in terminal) to start playing.

## 🚀 Deployment

To create a production build and run it locally:
```bash
npm run build
npm start
```
